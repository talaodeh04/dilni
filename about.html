About Dilni

Dilni is a national interactive platform designed to support the people of Palestine by offering real-time updates on critical areas, resources, and cultural destinations. It provides tools for users to report incidents, explore safe zones, and discover tourist locations across the country.

Our Vision

To empower communities through accessible technology that enhances safety, connectivity, and knowledge across all regions of Palestine.

Our Mission

  • Provide a reliable real-time map of key locations and updates
  • Allow users to report incidents with full details and media
  • Promote tourism through a categorized digital guide
  • Support emergency planning and communication

Technologies Used

  • Frontend: HTML, CSS, JavaScript
  • Map APIs: Leaflet.js, OpenStreetMap
  • Backend (optional future version): Node.js or Spring Boot
  • Database: Firebase or MySQL
